About Us

MEKS Coding and Robotic Club is a creative hub for young minds passionate about technology. We teach coding, robotics, and problem-solving skills to prepare students for the digital future.

Why This Matters for Our Students

  • Coding improves logical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving.
  • Robotics sparks curiosity and makes learning science and math practical.
  • Students gain digital confidence, ensuring they become creators, not just consumers.

Why This Matters for Your School

  • Position your school as a forward-looking institution with future-ready skills.
  • Raise your profile among parents eager to see their children excel in technology.
  • Provide opportunities for coding & robotics competitions that bring recognition and prestige.

What the Club Offers

Our curriculum is hands-on, exciting, and tailored for secondary school learners. Areas we cover include:

  • Web Development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
  • Game Development with Scratch, Kodu, and Python.
  • Robotics & Hardware Programming with Arduino.
  • App Development on beginner-friendly platforms.
  • Problem-Solving Challenges to sharpen critical thinking.

Each module ensures students don’t just memorize code but actually build projects—websites, apps, robots, and games. They leave with both knowledge and a portfolio of creations.

At MEKS Coding Club, we believe every child has the potential to invent, innovate, and inspire. By hosting the club at your school, you provide students with more than an extracurricular activity—you give them a launchpad into the future of technology.

We would be delighted to meet with you to discuss how the club can be integrated into your school. Together, we can empower the next generation of innovators.
